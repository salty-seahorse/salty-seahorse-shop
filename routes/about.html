Who We Are

We hand-craft locally-inspired soy candles made exclusively from repurposed glass in Orange Beach, AL.

The Salty Seahorse started as a happy hour conversation at our local tiki bar about finding a creative outlet and being our own bosses, but it has become much more.

We love where we live and are excited to be able to use what we do to give back to our community.

Candles for a Cause

When you buy from The Salty Seahorse, you get more than a candle. You get to be part of our mission to preserve the Alabama Gulf Coast and protect local marine and wildlife.

We donate a portion of proceeds from every candle to support local beach clean-up and marine/wildlife conservation efforts.

Recycled Bottles

All of our bottles are locally sourced from businesses and individuals in the Orange Beach/Gulf Shores area.

We’re proud to give these bottles a second life as candles and hope you’ll continue to re-use them after your candle is gone (e.g. start an herb garden, use to store pens and pencils, etc.).
